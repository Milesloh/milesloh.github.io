6.02 Notes

Power and Energy

Solar Car Energy

Solar cars use many types of energy

  • Kinetic Energy - anything that moves has kinetic energy
  • Light energy - any type of light (for example, sun light) has light energy
  • Chemical energy - anything that has chemicals for energy. (For example food, gas, batteries)
  • Gravitational Potential - the higher above ground the more Gravitational Potential Energy
  • Heat Energy - The warmer something is the more heat energy.
  • Electrical Energy - electrical things use electrical energy.

The unit for energy is J. J stands for joules. (Pronounced like jewels)
